Targets 14.1 REDUCE MARINE POLLUTION



By 2025, prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kins, in particular from land-based activities, including marine debris and nutrient pollution.

Vocabulary:

Marine Debris: Huge amounts of plastics, metals, rubber, paper, textiles, derelict fishing gear, derelict vessels, and other lost or discarded items enter the marine environment every day.

Nutrient Pollution: the process where too many nutrients, mainly nitrogen and phosphorus, are added to bodies of water and can act like fertilizer, causing excessive growth of algae.
