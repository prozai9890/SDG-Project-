Things to Do

  1. Find a Goal 14 charity you want to support. Any donation, big or small, can make a difference!

  2. Reduce waste-much of the waste that we produce on land ends up in the oceans. Stop using plastic bags: Usage and wrong disposal of plastic is a major cause of marine pollution.

  3. Organize a cleanup project for rivers and oceans. Engage your whole community to clean up a local river, seaside or an ocean.

  4. Never buy bottled water-boil, filter, chlorine, rainwater, do what you can.

  5. Run a campaign on the effects of plastic use on the seas and oceans.

  6. Buy local and certified fish. You can support small-scale producers by shopping in local markets and shops.
